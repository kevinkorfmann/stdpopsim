Welcome to stdvoidsim's documentation!
=======================================

``stdvoidsim`` is a library of population genetic simulation models for
Lovecraftian entities and eldritch horrors, built on the
`stdvoidsim <https://github.com/popsim-consortium/stdvoidsim>`_ framework.

Catalog at a glance
-------------------

The full :doc:`catalog <catalog>` lists all species and demographic models. By category:

- :ref:`Outer Gods & Great Old Ones <outer-gods-great-old-ones>`
